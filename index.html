About

Born in the heart of the kasi.

Food By Mr Buffalo (Amogelang Modisaotsile) serves up flame‑kissed meats, street‑style favourites, and premium plates that feel like home. Every dish is built on real flavour, proper portions, and respect for the culture.

From a small township hustle to a fully fledged brand in Schweizer-Reneke, we keep it authentic while levelling up the experience from the plating to the playlist.
